Welcome to My Portfolio

I am a senior at Miami University pursuing a Bachelor of Arts in Media and Communication with a Minor in Digital Marketing, maintaining a 3.87 GPA. My academic foundation spans coursework in User Experience Research, Social Media Marketing, Advertising, Communication Technology, and Media Economics — giving me both the strategic frameworks and the hands-on skills to execute across channels. I approach every project with analytical precision and creative intent, translating research and data into messaging that actually moves people. I hold certifications in Google Ads, Hootsuite, SEO, and ethical research, and I bring technical fluency with tools like Salesforce Marketing Cloud, Adobe Suite, Semrush, Canva, CapCut, and Google Workspace. Whether building a campaign from brief to launch or optimizing an existing funnel, I am committed to delivering work that is strategic, measurable, and brand-consistent. I am passionate about the intersection of storytelling and performance. This portfolio is a window into the work that reflects that passion.

Outside the classroom, I have gained real-world experience across brand, agency, and startup environments that have sharpened my instincts as a marketer. During my performance marketing internship at NinjaTrader, I contributed to the company's first international campaign launch post-acquisition with Kraken, presented customer segmentation strategy directly to the CMO, CEO, and President, and optimized 150+ multilingual emails across Salesforce Marketing Cloud. At Speed Lemon Media, I created platform-specific content, conducted market research, and supported press and branding efforts for clients. As a Fresh Prints Campus Manager, I have built relationships with 50+ student organizations and run targeted email and social campaigns to drive brand awareness and sales on campus. These experiences, combined with my involvement in Women in Marketing and PRSSA, have prepared me to contribute meaningfully to any marketing team from day one. I am excited to keep building on this foundation through future roles in digital and performance marketing.
